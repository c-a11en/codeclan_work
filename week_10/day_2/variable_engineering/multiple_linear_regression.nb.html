Volume of Users of a Rail Trail

Were going to build a model for volume. Can we explain the variation in volume using other predictors?

Our candidates: - temperature (Fahrenheit) - season - cloudcover (0 to 10) - precipitation (inches) - weekday

  • spring to fall are numbers rather than logical TRUE/FALSE
  • some collinear predictors (we want our predictors to be independent)

Variable Engineering

  • drop collinear columns
  • convert column types
  • clean up the column names
